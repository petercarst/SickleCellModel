Image Requirements

Upload Giemsa-stained peripheral blood smear microscopy images in JPEG, JPG, PNG, or WebP format. Higher resolution yields better accuracy.

How It Works

Images are preprocessed to 224×224 px, normalized, and fed to a CNN model served via TensorFlow Serving. Results are returned in real time.

Privacy

Images are processed transiently and are not stored on the server. Each request is independent and confidential.
